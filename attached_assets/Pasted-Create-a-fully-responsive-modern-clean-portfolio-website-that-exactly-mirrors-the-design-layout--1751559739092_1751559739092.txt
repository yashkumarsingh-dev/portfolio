## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

- **Fonts**:  
  - Headings: Montserrat or Poppins, bold  
  - Body: Inter, regular  
- **Color Palette**:  
  - Primary bg: `#1A1A1A`, sections alternate `#0F0F0F`  
  - Text: white (`#FFFFFF`), gray-300/500 accents  
  - Accent: neon cyan (`#00F0FF`) or purple (`#B728FF`)  
- **Spacing**:  
  - Sections: `py-24` (desktop), `py-16` (tablet/mobile)  
  - Containers: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-4`  

---

## 🎞️ ANIMATIONS

- **Framer Motion** (or GSAP) for:  
  - Section scroll‑in (fade‑up, slide)  
  - Hover states (cards, buttons)  
  - Hero text & arrow mount  
  - Count‑up on stats  
  - Carousel transitions  
- **Intersection Observer** triggers animations when elements enter viewport
